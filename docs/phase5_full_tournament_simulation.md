# Phase 5 — Full World Cup Simulation

The simulator now models the 2026 48-team format:

- 12 groups of 4 teams
- group-stage points table
- top 2 from each group qualify
- 8 best third-placed teams qualify
- Round of 32 knockout bracket
- champion projection

The UI includes a button to run up to **100,000 simulations** and returns:

- champion probability
- final probability
- semifinal probability
- quarterfinal probability
- Round of 32 probability

The bracket order is an explainable MVP approximation, not an official FIFA bracket reproduction.
